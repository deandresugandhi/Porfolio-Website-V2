About

I am Deandre Sugandhi, an aspiring full-stack web developer based in Sydney, Australia. I discovered programming when I was looking for ways to automate repetitive tasks that I do as a hobbyist musician, a process which immediately intrigued me. Since then, this newfound interest has led me to pursue and complete my IT degree in 2024.

When I'm not coding, my time is filled with movies, video games, books, and music. To me, stumbling across an unexpectedly well-crafted story is the definition of catharsis, no matter the media. Close the day off with an evening swim, and I've got myself the perfect day. Additionally, I am also developing a music and video production service with a fellow creative, taking gigs when opportunity arises.
